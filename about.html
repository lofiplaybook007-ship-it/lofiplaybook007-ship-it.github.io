About Lofi PLAYBOOK

Welcome to Lofi PLAYBOOK! If you are looking for the perfect atmosphere to study, work, or just unwind, you are in the right place.

Hi, I am Aryan Kumar Shah, the creator behind this website. Based in Kolkata, I have always been passionate about creating the perfect environment for deep focus and relaxation.

As a student and a creator managing multiple YouTube channels, I know firsthand how challenging it can be to concentrate during late-night study sessions or while working on complex coding projects. The right music and the right aesthetic can completely change your mindset.

That is exactly why I built Lofi PLAYBOOK. Whether you are prepping for big exams, working on a new video, or just trying to chill out, my goal is to share the best slowed + reverb tracks, aesthetic room tips, and gear recommendations to help you get in the zone.

Thanks for hanging out, and enjoy the vibes!
